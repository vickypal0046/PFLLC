Application Tracking System

Client is engaged in recruiting manpower across the globe. This company is India's largest manning firm providing highly skilled and trained Personnel for operations worldwide Presently, they administer in excess of 21000 Indian personnel under various global brands.

UK, India

  • Challenges
    • Managing the requisitions raised by companies.
    • Managing the Applicant's data.
    • Verification of candidates.
    • Scheduling of interviews.
    • Conducting Job fairs.
    • Assessments and Surveys.
    • Reporting and Analytics.
    • Data security and retention.
  • Key Initiative
    • Web Application for all business processes, hosted in Amazon Cloud for security, reliability and high availability.
    • Efficient Management of requisitions and easy Job postings on Website, that are linked to social media postings.
    • Application of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for predicting the eligibility of candidates.
    • Easy tracking of document requirements for different positions on different ships and Automated scanning of uploaded documents like passports.
    • Online scheduling and slot bookings for interview and Video conferencing for remotely located candidates .
    • Comprehensive dashboards and reports.
  • Business Impact
    • Reduction in Application processing time: Reduced from 3 hours to 20 minutes.
    • Better experience for the applicants: Average interviews conducted per month increased from 400 to 5000.
    • Better utilization of the Employees skills and time: The average time spent on processing one application has reduced by more than 40%.
    • Increase in Applicants numbers: The number of Applicants increased from 4876 per year in 2017 to 123414 applicant per year in 2021.
