Blockchain Development

A blockchain is a growing list of documents called "blocks" that are linked via encryption by a peer-to-peer network. A cryptographic hash of the preceding block, a timestamp, and transaction data are all included in each block. It's a distributed ledger network that's immune to data modification by design.
