Blockchain Development

A blockchain is a growing list of records called “blocks” linked by a peer-to-peer network, using cryptography. Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block, a timestamp, and transaction data. By design, it's a distributed ledger network resistant to modifying that data.
