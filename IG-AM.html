Identity Governance & Access Management

Identity Governance & Access Management

Our client brings thought leadership in implementation of IAM services. They provides specialized maintenance and extended levels of support while enabling users to access data and applications which enables productive IAM infrastructure to Banks, Financial institutions, and large enterprises.

USA

  • Challenges
    • The product that client had developed was an on premise IAM solution.
    • It was expensive and challenging to set up the application for each customer with their unique concerns around infrastructure and security.
    • They needed a solution that will allow them to expand and grow the business rapidly.
    • Maintenance was a big issue given multiple installations and different business rules.
  • Key Initiative
    • Converted the application to a Multitenant SAAS application.
    • Added features for secure communication with native on premise customer applications.
    • Enhanced the product with better user interface, security and upgraded performance.
  • Business Impact
    • Faster onboarding of new customers.
    • Enhanced experience for users.
    • Better Customer Support.
    • Tightened security and better performance.
